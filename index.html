By Order of the Onion

The State of the Onion is a solemn and highly unserious gathering devoted to the study, celebration, and competitive enjoyment of onions. Guests should expect expert presentations, ceremonial proceedings, onion-forward athletic contests, a dip competition, BBQ, awards, and the formal State of the Onion Address.

Attendees are encouraged to arrive with curiosity, good humor, and strong opinions about onion varieties.

🏛️

Official Proceedings

Opening ceremonies, presentation of the blooming onion, and formal recognition of onion-related excellence.

🎓

Expert Presentations

Distinguished speakers will address topics of urgent allium importance.
